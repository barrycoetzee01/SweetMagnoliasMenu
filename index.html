Sweet Magnolias Bake Shop

Omaha, Nebraska • Midtown + Millwork

Founded in 2016 by Katina Talley, Sweet Magnolias Bake Shop grew from a love of real baking and a decision to trade food science for flour, butter, and small-batch craft. With two Omaha locations, the shop is known for warm hospitality, handcrafted pastries, and simple ingredients you can actually pronounce.
